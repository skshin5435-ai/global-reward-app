Our Story

Sweetness with Purpose — The NXsweets Journey


NXsweets was founded in 2025 by Young Kim, a Type 2 diabetic for over 25 years. Having lived through the pain, struggles, challenges, and constant sacrifices that come with diabetes, Young knows firsthand how difficult it can be to enjoy life while maintaining a healthy diet.

In early 2025, by pure chance, Allulose found him — and it changed everything.  After learning about the incredible benefits of this natural, Zero Sugar, Low-calorie Allulose, Young realized this was more than a discovery — it was a calling.

Determined to help others living with diabetes, Young is on a mission to bring high-quality, affordable Allulose products to people worldwide. His goal is simple: to make life easier for diabetics and anyone seeking a healthier lifestyle, while sharing the importance of nutritional awareness — including Keto and Carnivore diets — as powerful tools to manage or even reverse diabetes and other ailments.

Our Mission

At NXsweets, our mission is to source the finest Non-GMO ingredients and produce the highest-quality, most affordable Allulose products for everyone. We believe that excellence is never an accident — it’s the result of vision, precision, and unwavering commitment.

Our journey is built upon a foundation of proprietary patents, a state-of-the-art fully automated manufacturing system, and an uncompromising dedication to quality. From the very beginning, we have sourced only the most exceptional ingredients from trusted origins — because great products can only come from great materials.

Every step of our process — from the meticulous selection of raw ingredients to the precision of our advanced manufacturing and careful final packaging — is guided by our promise to deliver purity, safety, and perfection.

Our Promise

Our challenge is our pride: to craft products that are not only safe and consistently superior, but that also inspire trust and bring joy to every customer we serve.

This is our promise — that NXsweets will continue to uphold the same high standards of quality, with unwavering commitment to safety, reliability, and taste, today and for generations to come.
